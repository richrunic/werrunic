II.

How a mark gets cut

Process

RUNIC is a small studio working across photography, modeling, and production — three crafts that are usually hired separately and rarely talk to each other.

We run all three under one roof so a shoot is planned, cast, shot, and delivered by people who were in the same room from day one. Fewer handoffs. Fewer things get lost in translation.

Based on-location and in-studio, working with brands, agencies, and independent talent who want work that holds up outside the feed it was made for.

  • 01
    Brief & cast
    We define the concept and, if needed, cast talent from our roster.
  • 02
    Plan & scout
    Locations, crew, schedule, and budget locked before anyone shows up.
  • 03
    Shoot
    Directed on set, with the same eye that planned it running the day.
  • 04
    Deliver
    Graded, sequenced, and handed off in the formats you'll actually use.
